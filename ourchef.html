Our Chef

Manuel Rodriguez is a globally renouned Chef and a Bronx native. After graduating from the Culinary Institute of America, Manuel began his culinary journey cooking at some of the globe's most renouned restaurants. Beginning his professional career at Chez Esta in Mexico City, Chef Rodriguez moved on to such Michelin rated restaurants as Le Meurice Paris, France; Le Djembe Dakkar, Senegal; Indian Accent New Delhi, India. With regard to global cuisine, Chef Rodriguez is one of the outright leaders of the "neo global-fusion movement" Both he, and we at Onze, are humbly grateful to call the borough of the Bronx our home.
