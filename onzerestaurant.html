Onze Restaurant

Onze is highly reflective of todays everchanging New York City, as well as the rapidly changing borough of the Bronx. Onze is located convenieintly close to Manhattan in the loft district of SOBRO. As New York City is a bastion of change and cultures, so is the cuisine at Onze. Akin to NYC and the borough of the Bronx, we are a rare fusion of American, European, Latin, Caribbean and West African cuisines. With fresh organic ingredients our cuisine and Chef are at the forefront of the "neo global-fusion movement" with regard to cuisine.
